Omkareshwar
Khandwa, Madhya Pradesh

Omkareshwar is a Hindu temple dedicated to God Shiva. It is located in Khandwa in Indian state Madhya Pradesh. It is one of the 12 revered Jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva. It is on an island called Mandhata or Shivapuri in the Narmada river. It is said that when seen from above, the island is shaped like the holy ‘Om’ symbol, which gives it the name Omkareshwar. The best way to reach Omkareshwar is to reach Indore via train and then hire a cab for Omkareshwar. The distance between Indore and Omkareshwar is about 90 Km and it takes about 2.5 hours to reach there. The entire road towards the place is enjoyable and interesting.

Shirdi
Ahmednagar, Maharashtra

Shirdi is a town in the state of Maharashtra, in western India. It's known as the former home of revered spiritual leader Sai Baba, and as a major pilgrimage site. It is one of the most popular religious places for Hindus in India and it attracts a lot of devotees every single year. Shirdi has started attracting a lot of tourists from all corners of the world. Shirdi attracts millions of devotees each year. The number of footfall rises during festivities. Shri Sai Baba Temple is one of the most popular pilgrim places in the country.

New Delhi
Delhi

New Delhi is the capital of India and a part of the National Capital Territory of Delhi. New Delhi has several tourist places. The India Gate is one of them. The monument was erected to mark the martyrhood of thousands of soldiers who gave their lives in the wars fought between 1914 to 1919. New Delhi has been a city with a lot of travellers pouring in and out for leisure as well as work. The National Capital is one of the important business and corporate centres with a lot of MNCs and industries in the Delhi-NCR.

Mahabaleshwar
Satara, Maharashtra

Mahabaleshwar is a hill station in India's forested Western Ghats range, south of Mumbai, located along the green Sahayadri range in Maharashtra’s Satara District. Though it might seem as if it is on a hill, Mahabaleshwar is on a plateau. History records the 1200s as the earliest that this place existed. Mahabaleshwar was always known as a holy place for the Hindus. This was primarily due to the origin of River Krishna. Many prominent rishis spent time here.

Dongargarh
Rajnandgaon, Chhattisgarh

Dongargarh is a Major town of india and municipality in Rajnandgaon District in the state of Chhattisgarh, India. It is a beautiful little town known for its scenic setting and the famous, hilltop Bambleshwari Temple. With stately mountains and lovely little ponds spread across its colorful patchwork of farmlands, meadows, rustic homes and snaking lanes, Dongargarh is much more than a temple town. It is a retreat into the lap of nature and away from the bustle of busy city lives. Derived from the words ‘dongarh’, meaning 'mountains', and ‘garh’ meaning 'fort', Dongargarh is full of hills and valleys, providing ample opportunities to revel in the beauty of nature.
